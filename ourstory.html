Promoted by DHAN Foundation

The success story of Koraput Cookies….

Koraput Cookies, originally started as fellowship project of a SBI Youth for India fellow in Semiliguda block, Koraput (Odisha) is now progressing further to become a self-sustainable enterprise in the region. With the support of DHAN Foundation some rural women of Koraput were brought together to form an SHG named ‘Koraput Millet Producer Group’. The objective of the SHG was to produce and sell value added millet-products to generate employment among the women.

The women with the support and encouragement by DHAN Foundation and SBI YFI Fellow started baking millet-cookies as their first product and that’s how the business ‘Koraput Cookies’ took off.

The business had faced multiple challenges in last year, from limitations of resources to second wave of COVID-19 hitting the region but the dedicated women continued to put their efforts to run the business and make it sustainable. There were a number of challenges including market competition, availability of infrastructure, volume of sales and lack of systematic procedure for running the enterprise but the hard-work and persistency paid off and the business started generating employment for the women of Koraput at a small scale.

Very inspired by the business model of ‘Shri Mahila Griha Udyog Lijjat Papad’, the group has its own set of values for running the enterprise related to transparency, quality, team-work and principles. The business has started small but with a broad vision to scale fast. The objective of the business continues to provide more employment to rural women and to provide healthy millet-products of Odisha to the nation.

After training the women to prepare the cookies, it was important to build their capacity, to enable them to continue the production independently. It took time and practice to reach at this level. With the support of DHAN Foundation, Koraput, regional coordinator Mr. Bijaya Kumar Nayak and SBI YFI Fellow, these women have been able to reach customers in multiple states of the nation including Delhi, Assam, Rajasthan Uttarakhand and Odisha. They are able to accept orders via the website www.koraputcookies.com and as well as retail outlets.

The product Koraput Cookies is being loved and supported by many due to its taste and health-benefits. Organic ragi (finger-millet), whole wheat flour, jiggery and nuts are major ingredients of the cookies and non-addition of sugar or any preservative makes it a exclusive product. The group and DHAN Foundation are now planning to expand the enterprise and scale it up further by increasing production capacity and providing employment to more women with the increasing volume of sales.
